About Me

Dr. Abid Latif Qureshi is a seasoned Nephrologist with over three decades of experience in clinical nephrology, dialysis, and kidney transplantation. He holds prestigious qualifications including M.B.B.S, F.C.P.S (Nephrology), and SCE (Nephrology, UK), and currently serves as Consultant Nephrologist at leading hospitals in Islamabad. He has served in senior roles across Pakistan and Saudi Arabia, including as Head of Nephrology at KRL Hospital. Dr. Qureshi is actively involved in academic activities, postgraduate training, and has contributed to several national and international medical conferences and publications.
